Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the detection?

Our AI model has achieved 94.68% accuracy in controlled tests, though real-world performance may vary depending on the complexity and context of the news article.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take privacy seriously. All analysis is performed in real-time and we don't store any article content or personal information.

How does the AI detect fake news?

Our AI analyzes multiple factors including writing style, source credibility patterns, and content consistency using advanced natural language processing techniques.

What types of content can I analyze?

You can analyze any news article text in English. The system works best with complete articles rather than headlines or short excerpts.
